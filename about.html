Who We Are

Founded in 2015, Yasheng has been dedicated to providing high-quality fiberglass and carbon fiber shell products to global customers. We have a professional team of materials scientists, engineers, and designers who focus on the research, development, and production of high-performance, lightweight composite material solutions.

Our products are widely used in various fields such as electronic devices, transportation, aerospace, and industrial equipment, offering customers exceptional protective performance and stylish design. With advanced production processes and a rigorous quality control system, we have earned the trust and collaboration of numerous internationally renowned brands.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class composite shell solutions that combine lightweight design, structural integrity, and cost-effectiveness. We believe every enclosure we produce should exceed our customer's expectations for quality and performance.
