FRP Radomes

Fiberglass reinforced plastic antenna covers designed for optimal signal transmission. Weather-resistant, lightweight, and engineered to withstand extreme environments from -40°C to +85°C.

  • Material: Fiberglass reinforced polymer (FRP/GFRP)
  • Frequency range: L, S, C, X, Ku band
  • Wall thickness: 2mm – 8mm (customizable)
  • Custom diameters: 200mm – 4000mm
  • UV-resistant gel coat finish standard
5G Antenna Radar Systems SatCom

USV Hulls

Custom fiberglass and carbon fiber hulls for unmanned surface vehicles. Optimized structural design for hydrodynamics, sensor integration, and payload capacity.

  • Material: FRP / CFRP / sandwich panel construction
  • Length range: 500mm – 5,000mm (max 5m)
  • Load capacity: 5kg – 300kg
  • Hull weight: from 3kg (lightweight options)
  • Mounting points for sensors & payload pre-drilled
Ocean Survey Maritime Patrol Last-Mile Delivery

Equipment Enclosures

Custom composite housings for industrial electronics, outdoor cabinets, and monitoring stations. Corrosion-resistant, lightweight, and thermally stable enclosures.

  • Material: FRP / CFRP / composite laminate
  • Wall thickness: 2mm – 10mm
  • Operating temperature: -40°C to +85°C
  • IP rating available: IP65 – IP68
  • EMI/RFI shielding options available
Industrial IoT Power Systems Telecom Base

Automotive Shells

Lightweight carbon fiber and fiberglass body panels, hoods, and aerodynamic shell components for low-volume automotive and motorsport applications.

  • Material: CFRP / GFRP / hybrid composite
  • Layup: vacuum infusion or prepreg available
  • Wall thickness: 1.5mm – 6mm (customizable)
  • Surface finish: gel coat or 2K paint ready
  • OEM & aftermarket shell compatible

About Us

Dongguan Yasheng FRP Technology Co., Ltd.

Founded in 2015, Yasheng has been dedicated to providing high-quality fiberglass and carbon fiber shell products to global customers. We have a professional team of materials scientists, engineers, and designers who focus on the research, development, and production of high-performance, lightweight composite material solutions.
