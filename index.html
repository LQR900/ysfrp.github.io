About Us

Dongguan Yasheng FRP Technology Co., Ltd.

Established in Dongguan, Guangdong Province, Yasheng FRP is a professional manufacturer specializing in fiberglass (FRP) and carbon fiber (CFRP) composite enclosures and structural parts.

Our 2,000+ square meter production facility is equipped with advanced molding, lamination, CNC cutting, and finishing lines. We serve clients in over 50 countries across telecommunications, maritime, automotive, and industrial sectors.
