Automotive Shell Components (Body Panels)

Lightweight carbon fiber (CFRP) and fiberglass (GFRP) body panels, hoods, fenders, and aerodynamic shell components for electric vehicles, motorsport, and classic car restoration projects.

Material OptionsCFRP / GFRP / Hybrid (carbon + glass)
Layup ProcessVacuum infusion / Prepreg autoclave / Hand layup
