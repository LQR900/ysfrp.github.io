FRP Radomes (Fiberglass Antenna Covers)

High-performance fiberglass reinforced plastic (FRP) antenna covers engineered for optimal signal transmission across all major frequency bands. Our radomes are manufactured using vacuum infusion or hand layup processes with UV-resistant gel coat finish for long-term outdoor durability.

Technical Specifications

MaterialFiberglass Reinforced Polymer (FRP / GFRP)
Diameter Range200mm – 4,000mm (custom sizes available)
Wall Thickness2mm – 8mm (depending on diameter & requirements)
Frequency BandsL, S, C, X, Ku band (customizable)
Signal Transmission>95% at operating frequency
Operating Temperature-40°C to +85°C (-40°F to +185°F)
Wind Load RatingUp to 250 km/h (155 mph)
Surface FinishUV-resistant gel coat (standard) / 2K paint (optional)
Color OptionsWhite (standard), RAL colors available on request
MOQ1 piece (prototype) / 5 pieces (production)
Request Quote WhatsApp

Applications

5G Base Stations

Low-loss radomes for 5G mmWave and sub-6 GHz antennas with minimal signal attenuation.

Radar Systems

Weather radar, air traffic control, and maritime surveillance radomes.

Satellite Communication

VSAT earth station and satellite dish protection covers.

Microwave Links

Point-to-point microwave link antenna protection enclosures.

Manufacturing Process

01

Design Review

Analyze your antenna specs, frequency requirements, and environmental conditions.

02

Mold Making

Create precision mold based on approved design drawings.

03

Layup & Curing

Vacuum infusion or hand layup with controlled resin-to-fiber ratio.

04

Trimming & Finishing

CNC trimming, surface finishing, and gel coat application.

05

QC & Delivery

Dimensional inspection, signal test, secure packaging, worldwide shipping.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

For standard radomes, MOQ starts from 5 pieces. For fully custom designs, we accept prototype orders of just 1 piece.

Can you match our existing antenna specifications?

Yes. Send us your antenna's technical drawing, frequency range, and dimensional requirements. Our engineering team will design a radome that fits perfectly.

What is the typical lead time?

Prototypes: 7–15 days. Production orders: 20–30 days depending on quantity and customization level.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We export to 50+ countries worldwide. We handle all documentation including CO, CI, PL, and packing lists.
