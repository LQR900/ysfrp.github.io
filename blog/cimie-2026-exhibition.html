Yasheng FRP Exhibits at CIMIE 2026

Mar 18, 2026 4 min read

From March 12–15, 2026, our team participated in the China International Marine Industry Exhibition (CIMIE) at the National Exhibition and Convention Center in Shanghai. It was our most successful exhibition yet, with over 340 visitors stopping by our booth to discuss FRP solutions.

What We Showcased

Our booth featured live samples across our four core product categories:

  • ▸FRP Radomes — 1.2m and 2.4m diameter telecom radome shells with integrated mounting systems
  • ▸USV Hulls — 3.5m and 6m USV hull prototypes demonstrating vacuum-infusion construction quality
  • ▸Equipment Enclosures — IP67-rated telecom cabinet samples with custom cutouts
  • ▸Automotive Shells — CFRP battery enclosure prototype for EV applications

Key Takeaways

Growing interest in USV solutions: Nearly 40% of our booth visitors were specifically interested in USV hulls, reflecting the rapid growth of the unmanned maritime market in Asia-Pacific.

Customization demand: Customers increasingly want fully customized enclosures — not just dimensions, but integrated mounting, cable management, and thermal management solutions.

Carbon fiber crossover: Several automotive and aerospace companies approached us about carbon fiber components, validating our investment in carbon fiber layup capabilities.

Thank You!

We'd like to thank everyone who visited our booth, attended our technical presentations, and connected with us during the exhibition. We're following up with all leads and look forward to turning these conversations into long-term partnerships.

If you missed us at CIMIE, feel free to reach out — we're happy to arrange a virtual or in-person consultation to discuss your FRP requirements.
