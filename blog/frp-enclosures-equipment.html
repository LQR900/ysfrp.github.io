How FRP Enclosures Protect Sensitive Equipment

Apr 5, 2026 5 min read

Sensitive electronic equipment — from telecom base stations to industrial control systems — faces a relentless enemy: the environment. Moisture, salt air, UV radiation, temperature extremes, and physical impact all threaten critical infrastructure. This is where FRP enclosures prove their worth.

Multi-Layer Protection

A well-designed FRP enclosure provides protection across multiple dimensions:

Weatherproof

IP65–IP67 rated sealing prevents water and dust ingress

UV Resistant

UV-stabilized gel coat prevents degradation under sunlight

Thermal Stability

Stable performance from -40°C to +80°C

EMI Shielding

Optional conductive coatings for electromagnetic isolation

Industry Applications

Telecom: 4G/5G base station equipment enclosures in coastal and desert environments, where metal enclosures would corrode or overheat.

Industrial Automation: Control cabinets for factories with high humidity, chemical exposure, or outdoor installation.

Rail & Transport: Onboard and wayside enclosures for signaling and communication systems.

Energy: Solar inverter enclosures, wind turbine component housings, and offshore platform equipment protection.

Custom Engineering

FRP enclosures can be custom-designed to exact specifications — including custom cutouts for connectors, cable glands, ventilation systems, and mounting provisions. This flexibility makes them ideal for OEM applications where standard metal enclosures won't fit.

Our engineering team works with customers from concept through production, including prototyping, testing, and mass production of custom FRP equipment enclosures.
