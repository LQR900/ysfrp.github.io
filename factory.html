Production Capabilities

Mold Making

In-house mold design and fabrication for custom enclosures. Aluminum, steel, and composite molds.

  • Custom mold design from CAD drawings
  • Mold life: 500+ cycles (aluminum)
  • Rapid prototyping molds available

Lamination / Layup

Vacuum infusion, hand layup, and prepreg processing for consistent fiber-to-resin ratio.

  • Vacuum infusion (primary method)
  • Prepreg autoclave for CFRP parts
  • Sandwich panel construction

CNC Trimming

Precision CNC trimming for clean edges and tight tolerances on every enclosure.

  • 3-axis CNC routing
  • Tolerance: ±0.5mm standard
  • Edge sealing & finishing

Surface Finishing

Gel coat application, sanding, priming, and paint-ready surface preparation.

  • UV-resistant gel coat (standard)
  • 2K automotive paint finish
    • Texture options available

Quality Inspection

100% dimensional check, visual inspection, and performance testing before shipment.

  • Dimensional tolerance verification
  • Surface defect detection
  • Load testing on request

Packaging & Shipping

Export-grade packaging with full documentation for worldwide delivery.

  • Wooden crate or palletized packing
  • CO, CI, PL documentation
  • Air freight or sea shipping
